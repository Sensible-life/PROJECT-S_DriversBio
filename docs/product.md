# Product Outline

## Product statement

DriveAI is a voice-first study coach for people who spend a lot of time driving and want to actively memorize, explain, and verify study material without touching a screen.

## Target user

- Frequently drives alone
- Studies from notes, lectures, books, or summaries
- Wants active recall instead of passive listening
- Needs hands-free interaction

## Core jobs to be done

1. Turn my notes into spoken quizzes.
2. Listen to my spoken explanation and fill missing parts.
3. Check whether what I said is true or misleading.
4. Remember weak concepts and bring them back later.

## MVP user stories

- As a user, I can paste study material and save it as a study source.
- As a user, I can start a driving study session from my phone.
- As a user, I can hear one question at a time and answer by voice.
- As a user, I can receive short spoken feedback about correctness and missing points.
- As a user, I can review weak points after the session ends.

## Non-goals for MVP

- Full autonomous in-car assistant integration
- Long free-form tutoring conversations
- OCR-heavy book ingestion pipeline
- Social or multiplayer study features
